Your protection is our priority. We offer a wide range of insurance services to give you confidence and security.

Home Insurance: Protect Your Dream Home

Covers damage or loss to a person’s home and belongings

Typically includes coverage for:

- Dwelling (the physical structure of the home)

- Personal property (furniture, appliances, etc.)

- Liability (accidents or injuries on the property)

- Additional living expenses (if the home becomes uninhabitable)

Policyholders can choose from various coverage options, such as:

- Actual cash value (ACV) or replacement cost value (RCV)

- Special coverage for valuable items (e.g., jewelry, artwork)

- Endorsements for specific perils (e.g., flood, earthquake)

Auto Insurance: Drive with Confidence · Covers damage or loss to a vehicle and protects the driver and passengers · Typically includes coverage for: - Collision (accidents with other vehicles or objects) - Comprehensive (non-collision events, such as theft or vandalism) - Liability (bodily injury or property damage to others) - Personal injury protection (PIP) or medical payments - Uninsured/underinsured motorist coverage · Policyholders can choose from various coverage options, such as: - Different levels of liability coverage - Special coverage for roadside assistance or rental cars

Business Insurance: Protect Your Business and Assets · Covers damage or loss to a business and its assets · Typically includes coverage for: - Property (buildings, equipment, inventory, etc.) - Liability (accidents or injuries on the business premises) - Business interruption (loss of income due to business closure) - Workers’ compensation (injuries or illnesses suffered by employees) - Cyber insurance (data breaches or cyber attacks) · Policyholders can choose from various coverage options, such as: - Different levels of liability coverage - Special coverage for specific industries or risks (e.g., product liability)
